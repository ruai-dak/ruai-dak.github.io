The Power of Simplicity

By Ruai Yiey • June 23, 2025

Simplicity isn't about lacking features. It's about clarity. In design, the simpler the interface, the more powerful the user feels.

Understanding the Core

Every great design strips away the unnecessary to focus on the essential. Think Google. Think Apple. Think Medium.

“Perfection is achieved not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away.” — Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

Focus on your content. Let whitespace guide the reader. Let typography do the talking. And most importantly — remove what doesn't matter.
